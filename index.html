// HOW PATCHES WORK

  1. FIRST SHIFT — earned at first check-out from any partner.
  2. BIKE COMMUTE — earned when you tick "bike" on transport at check-in.
  3. LOTE PLACEMENT — earned for any shift conducted in a language other than English.
  4. NIGHT SHIFT — earned for any check-in that starts after 18:00.
  5. SOS CLAIM — earned when you claim and complete a partner SOS before its deadline.
  6. DRIFTER — earned for three shifts at partners you'd never visited before.

Patches are personal achievements only. They do not unlock features, are not visible to partner organisations, and never appear on a leaderboard.

// HOW THE SCRAPBOOK WORKS

  1. AUTO-COMPILED from your postcards (chronological), reflection cards, hours-by-partner, and patches earned.
  2. SNAPSHOTS every Sunday at 18:00 when the week's edition closes — scroll back through prior weeks at any time.
  3. BLANK PAGES — three at the back for handwritten notes you photograph and upload.
  4. PRINT-READY at the end of the semester (Wk 12) as an A5 stapled booklet PDF, generated server-side via Paged.js.
  5. PRIVATE — only you and the unit team see your scrapbook. A separate cohort scrapbook is exported with consenting postcards only.

The scrapbook serves as the unit's reflective-assessment artefact for MON2000.
