// HOW PATCHES WORK

  1. FIRST SHIFT — earned at first check-out from any partner.
  2. BIKE COMMUTE — earned when you tick "bike" on transport at check-in.
  3. LOTE PLACEMENT — earned for any shift conducted in a language other than English.
  4. NIGHT SHIFT — earned for any check-in that starts after 18:00.
  5. SOS CLAIM — earned when you claim and complete a partner SOS before its deadline.
  6. DRIFTER — earned for three shifts at partners you'd never visited before.

★ Patches are tactile reminders, not currencies. They do not unlock features, are not visible to partner organisations, and never appear on a leaderboard. They live only in your scrapbook.
